About Us

What is KazLing?

We are an informal union of past competitors, who often serve as jury members and problem setters for Kazakhstan's National Linguistics Olympiad, which is organized by the Republican scientific and Practical Center "Daryn". Our decision to form KazLing as it is, has been driven by the desire to:

  1. Provide open offical resources for linguistics olympiad preparation in Kazakhstan
  2. Make the existing competition self-sustainable by establishing native problem creation
  3. Enhance the selection process and the preparation of the national team
